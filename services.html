Services

At Oak Hill Speech Therapy, I offer a comprehensive range of services tailored to meet the unique needs of each child. My offerings include individual in-person therapy sessions, flexible teletherapy options, and valuable staff training resources.

My in-person therapy sessions, available in 30 or 45-minute intervals, are designed to provide personalized attention to your child. These sessions can conveniently take place in the comfort of your child's home or private school, ensuring a familiar and comfortable environment for optimal learning.

For those who prefer or require a more flexible approach, my real-time, face-to-face teletherapy sessions offer a practical solution. These sessions provide the same high-quality therapy as my in-person sessions, but with the added convenience of flexible scheduling and the ability to participate from any location.

In addition to my direct therapy services, I also offer staff training and workshops. These sessions can be a valuable resource for teachers and staff members, providing them with the tools and knowledge to better support students with communication disorders. I also offer reduced rate screenings and consultations as part of this service.

At Oak Hill Speech Therapy, my goal is to provide effective, personalized, and convenient therapy solutions to help each child reach their full communication potential.

What Speech-Language Therapy Will Do For Your Child

Toddlers and Preschoolers Will Improve:

  • Speech clarity
  • Vocabulary
  • Ability to put together longer sentences
  • Play skills
  • Social interaction
  • Ability to answer questions
  • Ability to follow directions

School-Aged Children Will Improve:

  • Auditory processing and classroom listening
  • Vocabulary skills
  • Linguistic complexity and organization in speaking and writing
  • Describing and defining skills
  • Understanding figurative language, humor, and sarcasm
  • Word retrieval
  • Articulation
